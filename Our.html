Authentic Israeli

Georgian Warmth


At Supra Hummus we believe that when it’s tasty, it’s simply irresistible. Located
in the heart of Tbilisi, our bistro is a warm meeting point between Israeli
tradition and Georgian hospitality — an authentic place where flavors and
                          cultures unite around the love for hummus.


We take great pride in preparing classic, creamy hummus, made daily from the
freshest ingredients, inspired by the rich culinary heritage of the Middle East.
Our hummus is more than just food — it’s an experience, a reason to gather,
                                                 share, and enjoy.


Alongside our famous hummus, you’ll find fresh, crispy falafel packed with flavor,
and a rich, perfectly cooked shakshuka that brings the taste of Middle Eastern
                                         mornings straight to your table.


At Supra Hummus, every bowl of hummus tells a story — one of authenticity,
simplicity and warmth. Whether you’re looking for the best hummus in Tbilisi,
delicious falafel or a taste of Middle Eastern cuisine, our dishes will make you feel
                                                    right at home.
